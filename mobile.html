Mobile App

For this project we had to come up with our own idea that relates to Mobile Media and Mixed Reality such as AR and VR.

I came up with the idea to create a mobile app that would show avaliable off street parking in the Wellington CBD called the Park Finder App.

Launch Campaign

As part of the project we had to come up with a launch campaign plan which explains how we will showcase our product.

My main focus was to use Psychographic and Persuasive Design to encourage my target audience to use the Park Finder App.

App Prototype

As part of the project we had to create a prototype.

I created this rough high fidelity prototype of how the Park Finder app works. I used Figma to create my app to give people an idea of how it would function.
